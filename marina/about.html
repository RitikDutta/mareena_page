About Us

Message from the CEO:

In the legacy of Late Mr. Sunder Keswani Ji, we honour his memory and the guiding principles he left us with:.

During the years when Marina Electric General Store thrived from 1975 to 2018, we learned that "big success does not come without significant challenges." As we continue our journey in 2021, we remember that "we are social beings. Let us embrace 'Be Indian, buy Indian.' Let's strive for simplicity and clarity in our language. Our focus should be on addressing the real issues, concentrating on where we can make the most meaningful impact. Be personal in our approach and always look forward, not back." As the CEO, I embrace the responsibility to ensure each member of our team reaches their highest potential. To achieve this, we must empower individuals to surpass their own expectations. My role is to be omnipresent in serving our employees, going beyond merely providing the tools they need. In addition, I want to emphasize our commitment to kindness not only toward one another but also toward animals. Nature holds valuable lessons for us, and we must remember that we learn with every movement and practice with every experience. Let's carry these values forward and continue to make Marina World a place of growth, innovation, and harmony in the world of maritime excellence.

It starts in your mind; we become what we think. Think big to achieve greatness.

Bonton Cables

RoHS compliant wires and cable manufacturing facility with ISO 14001 & OHSAS 18000 certifications. Bonton has a distinguished history in wires and cable industry. The company is pioneer in manufacturing eco-friendly and specialised cables which are accoladed for weather resistant, distortion free signaling and special bending radius that can perform at temperatures ranging from -50°C to 950°C.

Polycab

Polycab is a leading company in the electrical industry with a glorious track record of over 4 decades. It has the reputation of being the fastest growing company in the Indian Cables sector.Polycab is engaged in the business of manufacturing and selling wires and cables and fast moving electrical goods ‘FMEG’ under the ‘POLYCAB’ brand. Apart from wires and cables, we manufacture and sell FMEG products such as electric fans, LED lighting and luminaires, switches and switchgear, solar products and conduits & accessories.
